ABOUT US

‘Monarch College of Art and Technology’ is the root of Art and Animation Technology. This college was established in the year 2004 and continuously scoring outstanding records all over India. Our students are currently associated with career related to 2D, 3D, modelling, gaming. We take immense pride in showcasing our remarkable achievement of our student’s outstanding contributions to the iconic world of animation at Walt Disney. It is affiliated to University of Kalyani ,Nadia,West Bengal. We have a good connection with several eminent animation companies such as Walt Disney, Prana, Symbiosis Technologies, HCL Technologies, Paperboat, MPC, Toonz Animation, Foundation Pixel Animations Pvt. Ltd., Reliance Animation, Laughing Dots, iCat, Green Gold, Aeon, Gameshastra, Animatzione, DQ Entertainment, Mad Angles, Pixalot, Aplus Studios and Side FX Entertainment. Today the ‘Monarch College of Art and Technology’ is acclaimed as one of the finest educational, art and animation institute.

A few words

About the Director

As Walt Disney said ‘Animation can explain whatever the mind of man can conceive. This facility makes it the most versatile and explicit means of communication yet devised for quick mass appreciation’, it is quite evident that animation is an art form that reflects the creativity of an artist. Thus, there are many people around the world who are intrigued to learn this form of art to implement in the vast world of creativity. Mr. Mriganka Mandal is an artist and animator, who studied from Sargachi Ramakrishna Mission. He studied BVA in Indian college of Arts and Draftsmanship from Rabindra Bharati University. He also did the postgraduation with MFA from Indira Kala Sangit Vishwavidyalaya. Previously, he worked as an animator and project leader at Padmalaya Telefilms Limited and Assistant Animator Heart Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. in Hyderabad. He has worked with several renowned companies such as Warner Brothers, Startoon, Fancy Monkey studios and TLC. Apart from this, he was also involved with national and international seminars, workshops and exhibitions. He is the guest lecturer in Department of Visual Arts, K.U. Mr. Mriganka Mandal is the founder and principal of ‘Monarch College of Art and Technology’ in Berhampore, West Bengal. This college is affiliated under the University of Kalyani and it was founded in the year 2004. It is one of the best colleges that cater students who are willing to learn animation and multimedia all over India. Since the last 21 years, MCAT has been offering BVA degree course (four years) and diploma courses to students who are creative and passionate with art and drawing. .
