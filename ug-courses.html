    Duration :-2 years

    Eligibility :-Graduate in any stream, Candidate with any degree or diploma in fine arts or computer application will have a preference.

  • 2) Post Graduate Diploma in Animation Film Making

    Duration :-4 Years [ 6 semester + 2 Semester ] (entrance)

    Eligibility :-The basic qualification for the course is 10+2 equivalent to passed .

  • 3) Bachelor in Ceramic Design

    Duration :-4 Years [ 6 semester + 2 semester ] (entrance)

ABOUT THE SUBJECT

  • Bachelor in Animation and Film Making

    • Sem ( l & ll ) -Compulsory Bengali, Compulsory English, Environmental Studies,History of Animation, Principle & Terminology Sculpture, Computer Multimedia. of Animation, Conceptual Art, Modelling & Sculpture, Computer Multimedia.
    • Sem ( III & IV ) - History of Animation, Computer Animation, Classical Animation, Computer Graphic Design, Project-1
    • Sem ( V & VI ) -History of Animation, Experimental Animation, Game Design, Project-2
    • Sem ( VII & VIII ) -Entrance

  • Bachelor in Animation and Film Making

    • Sem ( l & ll ) -Art & Techniques of Cinema, Advanced Digital Animation, Advanced Classical Animation, Project-1
    • Film Direction, Compositing & VFX, Seminar, Project-2 with entrance.

  • Bachelor in Ceramic Design

    • Sem ( l & ll ) -Compulsory Bengali, Compulsory English, Environmental Studies,History of Pottery, Computer Design, Preparation of Clay Bodies & Glaze, Drawing & Painting, Fabrication.
    • Sem ( III & IV ) - Method of Ceramic, Application of Glaze & firing, Pottery, Composition, 3d Modeling & Design, Project-1.
    • Sem ( V & VI ) -Materials of Ceramic, Industrial Ceramic Design, Advanced Computer Design, Project-2
    • Sem ( VII & VIII ) -Entrance
